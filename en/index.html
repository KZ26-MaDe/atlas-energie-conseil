Energy & decision FAQ

Key questions before deciding

Before committing to a solar, storage or hybrid project, some questions need to be clarified. These answers help you understand the stakes before a first discussion.

When does a solar project become relevant for a business or site in Morocco?

A solar project becomes relevant when the consumption profile, electricity costs, available roof or land area and site constraints make coherent self-consumption possible. The analysis should review profitability as well as technical and operational limits.

Is a battery always necessary with a photovoltaic installation?

No. A battery is relevant only if it serves a clear usage profile: shifting energy, reducing certain peaks, improving flexibility or strengthening resilience. It should be sized around the site's real needs, not added automatically.

What is the difference between solar self-consumption and battery storage?

Self-consumption means directly using the solar electricity produced on site. Storage keeps part of that energy for another time or a specific usage profile. The two approaches can complement each other, but they do not answer the same need.

Why carry out an energy analysis before requesting quotes?

An independent analysis helps clarify objectives, compare scenarios and avoid basing the decision only on a commercial offer. It frames the project before approving a budget, selecting a partner or planning deployment.

How can the power supply of a sensitive or industrial site be secured?

Power continuity depends on critical loads, sensitivity to outages, grid conditions, and the possible role of a battery or generator. A structured approach compares backup and resilience scenarios before a decision.

Does Atlas Energie Conseil install solar panels or batteries?

No. Atlas Energie Conseil acts as an independent advisor. The aim is to analyze, compare, structure and secure energy decisions. The support is not based on selling or supplying equipment.

Who does Atlas Energie Conseil support?

The support is designed for businesses, homeowners with structured projects, farms, hotels, schools, service-sector sites, industrial companies, developers, investors and project owners who want to clarify a solar, storage or energy performance decision in Morocco.

What happens during the first exchange?

The first exchange is used to understand the site context, objectives, constraints, project maturity and decisions to secure. It identifies useful next steps without immediately committing to a full study.

What information is useful to analyze an energy site?

Useful information may include electricity bills, consumption profiles, site constraints, available roof or land area, continuity objectives, offers already received and investment priorities. The information available helps define the right level of analysis.

Does the pre-diagnostic replace a technical study?

No. The pre-diagnostic provides indicative first guidance. It does not replace a complete technical, economic or financial study. It prepares a more structured exchange and identifies the scenario to explore.
